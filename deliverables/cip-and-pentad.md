# CIP and Pentad Integration Support

Push for community agreement and resolution of key DevX and DeFi CIPs that will unlock significant developer experience improvements: standardization of CBOR objects, Account Address Enhancement (CIP-159), nested transactions (CIP-118), and programmable tokens (CIP-113). Beyond advocacy and consensus-building, this deliverable includes propagating changes post-acceptance by helping the community update tooling and libraries to support new features. It also covers supporting Pentad integrations (Stablecoins, Bridges, Oracles) to ensure these critical infrastructure pieces provide an excellent developer onboarding experience.
