# Developer Outreach

A multi-channel developer outreach program designed to grow the Cardano developer community and provide ongoing support to builders. This deliverable covers three key channels: community management through Discord, developer engagement through hackathons and build clubs, and educational content through live streams and video tutorials. The goal is to create multiple touchpoints where developers — especially newcomers — can get help, learn, and connect with the community, ultimately reducing the feeling of isolation that often accompanies building on a less populated ecosystem.
