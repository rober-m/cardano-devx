# Community Alignment and Collaboration

The most important deliverable of the strategy: aligning the ecosystem around a shared DevX vision. This involves mapping all ecosystem tooling to create a comprehensive baseline, identifying gaps and overlaps, and establishing a shared understanding of the current state of developer experience across Cardano. The team will actively contribute to community tooling with an emphasis on off-chain and integration libraries, improve existing DApp development tooling (LSPs, extensions, TUIs, etc.), and coordinate with various companies and projects to ensure efforts are complementary rather than duplicated. The outcome is a clear roadmap of the ecosystem's developer experience backed by a list of companies and projects committed to executing it.
